A visual exploration of sucidal ideation.

Reference images (end), representative of the various facets of suicide and suicidal ideation, were collaged to create compositions. These compositions were then used as collage material fed to the subsequent itteration. Each composition – each itteration exploring the gravity, melancholia, darkness, and beauty of and within those suffering from suicidal ideation.
